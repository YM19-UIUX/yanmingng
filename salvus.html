Salvus

Using Artificial Intelligence To Create A Safe Social Space On TikTok

Date Published: 7 March, 2025
[1 min read]

The Project

Leverage a modern technology to address a real-world societal problem.

Team Dynamics:
🚀 A diverse, multi-disciplinary team spanning Computer Engineering, Environmental Engineering, English Humanities, and Business Marketing.

What We Delivered:
📌 A visually compelling A3 poster that captured our research and solution.
🎤 An oral presentation to effectively communicate our findings and impact.

Timeline:
One semester to research, ideate, and deliver a meaningful solution.

The Process

Through extensive research, we discovered a growing issue on TikTok—the rise of malicious comments that contribute to unhealthy habits. While TikTok has built-in comment control mechanisms, these come with limitations.

Seeing the need for a proactive solution, our team leveraged Artificial Intelligence (AI) to develop Salvus—Latin for "safe." Salvus is an AI-powered content filter designed to detect and block harmful comments before they are posted, reducing exposure to negativity in the first place.

How It Works:
✔️Real-time content detection – Salvus analyzes text input before posting.
✔️AI-powered filtering – Built on a perceptron-based artificial neural network, mimicking human decision-making to classify data accurately.
✔️Autonomous & scalable – Functions with minimal human intervention, making moderation more efficient.

Final Product

🎨 A visually appealing design with clear and coherent flow that let visuals do the talking.
✅ No unnecessary details, just meaningful insights.
🔤 A classy serif font for titles and subheadings, paired with an easy-to-read sans-serif for the main content.
👀 A soft, eye-friendly color palette for a polished, professional look.

This final product wasn’t just a presentation of our ideas—it was a reflection of our dedication to creating something meaningful and effective together.
